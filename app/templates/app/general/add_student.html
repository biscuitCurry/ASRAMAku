Form Guide
  • Full Name & Course: Enter the student's legal name and official course abbreviation (e.g., Diploma in Computer Science).
  • Session: Type the intake batch. The box will auto-format to uppercase with dashes (e.g., typing juldis2026 becomes JUL - DIS 2026).
  • Matric ID & IC Number: Enter the matric & IC number.
  • RFID UID: Click into the input field and scan the student's IC using the USB reader to capture the chip serial number.
  • TVETMARA Email: Enter the official institutional email assigned to the student (e.g., student@sgpetani.tvetmara.edu.my).
  • Parent Contact: Provide the active phone number of the parent or legal guardian for emergency or checkout alert tracking.
